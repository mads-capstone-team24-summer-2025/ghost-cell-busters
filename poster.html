üéØ Abstract & Objectives

This project analyzes single-cell and spatial transcriptomics data from human breast cancers to distinguish tumor and normal cell populations through comprehensive computational methods. Our workflow integrates data acquisition, feature engineering, copy number variation analysis, machine learning classification, and therapeutic target identification using DGIdb and Open Targets databases.

Key Goals

  • Precision Classification: Develop interpretable ML models to distinguish tumor vs normal cells
  • Feature Engineering: Extract biologically meaningful signatures from scRNA-seq data
  • Therapeutic Discovery: Identify clinically relevant gene targets for precision oncology
  • Cross-Dataset Validation: Ensure model generalizability across multiple breast cancer cohorts
